Help Center & FAQ

Learn more about the probation assessment guidelines and evaluation rubrics.

search

What do the evaluation dimensions represent? expand_more

Technical Efficiency: Quality of code, design choices, speed of implementation, and adherence to codebase architectures.
Ability to Learn: Onboarding speed, uptake of new systems/technologies, response to coaching, and knowledge growth.
Ability to Adapt: Flexibility under shifting requirements, teamwork in changing structures, and problem-solving velocity.
Attitude: Professional collaboration, willingness to help others, positive workspace impact, and attendance.

How is the overall probation score calculated? expand_more

Each month's evaluation score is the simple arithmetic mean of the four core metrics: (Tech + Learn + Adapt + Attitude) / 4. The final score is the cumulative average of all completed months.

What is the minimum score to pass probation? expand_more

Associates are typically expected to maintain an average score of 3.5 or above by the end of their probation period (usually 4 months) to receive a positive confirmation. Scores below 3.0 flag the employee as "Needs Improvement".
